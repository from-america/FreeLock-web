Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

FreeLock ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1 Data We Collect

On-Device Data:

  • • NFC card identifiers (stored as SHA-256 hashes only)
  • • App blocking preferences and configurations
  • • Lock schedules and settings
  • • Audit log of lock/unlock events
  • • Emergency passcode (stored as SHA-256 hash)
  • • Optional accountability contact email

No data collection:

FreeLock does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of this data to our servers. Everything stays on your device.

2 How We Use Your Data

  • • On-device processing: Your NFC card identifiers are hashed locally and compared on-device for authentication
  • • Local storage: All data is stored locally in the iOS Keychain and app private storage
  • • No sharing: We never share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties
  • • No analytics: We do not track your app usage or behavior

3 Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • • iOS Keychain encryption for sensitive data (tokens, passcode, contacts)
  • • SHA-256 hashing of NFC card identifiers (original values never stored)
  • • On-device only storage with no cloud backup
  • • Device-level encryption provided by iOS

4 Third-Party Services

Apple Screen Time API: FreeLock uses Apple's FamilyControls and ManagedSettings frameworks to block apps. Apple does not receive information about which apps you block. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Accountability Notifications (Optional): If you configure an accountability contact, emergency unlock events may be sent to our notification service. This includes only:

  • • An anonymized device identifier (hash)
  • • The event type (e.g., "emergency_unlock")
  • • Timestamp
  • • Your accountability contact's email address

No other data is transmitted. The accountability feature is entirely optional.

5 Your Privacy Rights

Since all your data is stored on your device:

  • • You have full control and ownership of your data
  • • You can delete tokens anytime from Settings
  • • You can export your audit log in text format
  • • You can use Emergency Delete to remove all data
  • • You can uninstall the app to remove all data

6 Children's Privacy

FreeLock is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of FreeLock after updates means you accept the changes.

8 Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at FreeLock@fromamerica-llc.com.

Bottom Line:

Your data is yours and yours alone. We don't collect, store, or share your data. FreeLock is 100% private by design.
