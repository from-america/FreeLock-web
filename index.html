How It Works

Three simple steps to reclaim your time.

1

Register a Card

Open FreeLock and scan any NFC-enabled card. This becomes your physical key.

2

Select Apps to Block

Choose the apps that distract you the most. Social media, games, or news apps.

3

Lock & Focus

Tap "Lock". The apps are now inaccessible. To use them again, you must physically tap your card to the back of your phone.
